Coding Tutorials Introduction

These set of tutorial pages were made by members of our Eastside Catholic Coding Club for the purpose of helping anyone that is either involved in Coding Club itself, or is just curious about Computer Science and is willing to put in the effort to learn how to code. Take into consideration that these tutorials were created by students and teachers for the purpose of educating other students and teachers so do not expect an extremely complicated series of tutorials. The tutorials were instead designed to be understood by even beginning coders.
